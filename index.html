Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the treatment really painless?+
Yes. We use modern anaesthesia and a gentle approach for all procedures, including root canals and implants, so most patients feel little to no discomfort.
Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?+
Walk-ins are welcome, but booking an appointment ahead of time means shorter waiting and a dedicated time slot for your treatment.
How long does a dental implant take?+
Implant treatment is done in stages over a few months to allow proper healing, with the number of visits depending on your specific case.
What are your clinic timings?+
We're open daily, Monday to Sunday, from 10:00 AM onward. Call ahead for the exact closing time on the day you plan to visit.
Do you treat children?+
Yes, we offer gentle dental check-ups and treatment for children in a comfortable, friendly environment.
